## With Screenshots

**Recommended images to include:**

1. **Homepage/Landing page** - Shows the clean interface
2. **Dashboard** - Shows wallet connection and balance
3. **Send payment form** - Shows the payment interface
4. **Transaction history** - Shows completed transactions
5. **Multi-sig vault page** - Shows advanced feature
6. **Admin metrics dashboard** - Shows analytics

**How to take screenshots:**
1. Open your app in browser
2. Use browser's full-page screenshot (or use a tool like Awesome Screenshot)
3. Crop to show the important parts
4. Upload to Twitter with your post
